Scholarships for Higher Education in Andhra Pradesh

# Scholarship Name Details
1

Jagananna Vidya Deevena Scheme

For Providing Financial Funds for Study ie Full Fee Reimbursement.

for eligible students who are pursuing B.Tech, M.Tech, B.Pharmacy, M.Pharmacy, B.Ed, ITI, MBA, MCA and Other Degree and PG Courses
2

Jagananna Vasathi Deevena Scheme

Financial Aid for Hostel Fee.

for eligible students who are pursuing B.Tech, M.Tech, B.Pharmacy, M.Pharmacy, B.Ed, ITI, MBA, MCA and Other Degree and PG Courses
3

YSR Vidyonnathi Scheme

Free Civil Services Coaching.

for SC, ST, BC, KAPU, Brahmin, Minority and EBC Students.
4

YSR Jagananna Videshi Vidya Deevena Scheme

Financial Aid for Higher Education in Abroad.

for KAPU, OC, BC, EBC students who belongs to low income family.
